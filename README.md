# Countdown

Track your most anticipated game releases, movie premieres, and TV series finales with style! Fuel the anticipation for major blockbusters, next-gen titles, and upcoming seasons.

- 🕒 Track upcoming releases: Manage countdowns for your most hyped entertainment.
- 🎨 Sleek glassmorphism UI: Fast, elegant, and native to Nextcloud.
- 🏗️ Custom sizes (S/M/L): Highlight your most anticipated titles.
- 📊 HUD Widgets: Track your release questline at a glance on the dashboard.
- 🔔 Day-One notifications: Get alerts when the wait is finally over.
- 🌗 Dark & Light modes: Matches your Nextcloud theme.
